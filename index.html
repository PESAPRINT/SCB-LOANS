Terms and Conditions

1. Loan Agreement

By accepting these terms, you enter into a legally binding agreement with SCB Quick Loans (Kenya) Limited, a company licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya.

2. Loan Amount and Interest

The loan amount and interest rate will be displayed in your personalized offer. Interest is calculated on a reducing balance basis at rates between 6.5% and 9% per month, depending on your credit profile.

3. Processing Fee

A non-refundable processing fee is required to cover:

  • Credit Reference Bureau (CRB) checks - KSh 50
  • Identity verification and fraud prevention - KSh 75
  • M-Pesa transaction costs - KSh 35
  • Administrative processing - remaining amount

4. Repayment

You agree to repay the loan according to the terms selected. Late payments may result in:

  • Late payment fees as per Central Bank guidelines
  • Listing with Credit Reference Bureaus
  • Legal action for recovery

5. Early Repayment

You may repay the loan early at any time with no penalty. Interest will only be charged for the days you've used the money.

6. Data Protection

Your personal data is collected, stored, and processed in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. We use your data to verify your identity, assess creditworthiness, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Default

If you default on repayment, we may:

  • Report the default to Credit Reference Bureaus
  • Charge default interest as permitted by law
  • Instruct collection agents to recover the debt

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Any disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Nairobi.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Full name and ID number
  • M-Pesa registered phone number
  • Transaction history and statements
  • Device information and IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Identity verification and fraud prevention
  • Credit assessment through CRB checks
  • Loan processing and disbursement
  • Customer service and communication
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

3. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Credit Reference Bureaus for credit checks
  • Safaricom for M-Pesa verification
  • Regulatory authorities as required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with access controls
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to processing of your data

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, typically up to 6 years after your last interaction.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at:
Email: dpo@scbquickloans.co.ke
