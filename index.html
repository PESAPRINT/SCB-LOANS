Terms and Conditions

1. Fair Lending: We adhere to Central Bank of Kenya guidelines on responsible lending. Our interest rates are transparent with no hidden fees.

2. Flexible Repayment: You can choose repayment periods from 1 to 12 months. Early repayment incurs no penalties - you only pay interest for days utilized.

6. Data Protection: Your data is encrypted and protected under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

7. Cooling Off Period: You have 14 days to cancel without penalty as per Kenyan consumer protection laws.
