Terms and Conditions

1. Loan Agreement

By accepting these terms, you enter into a legally binding agreement with SCB Quick Loans (Kenya) Limited, licensed by the Central Bank of Kenya.

2. Loan Amount and Interest

Interest is calculated on a reducing balance at 6.5%–9% per month depending on your credit profile.

3. Processing Fee

A non-refundable processing fee covers CRB checks, identity verification, and administrative costs.

4. Repayment

Late payments may result in late fees and CRB listing.

5. Early Repayment

You may repay early with no penalty. Interest is charged only for days used.

6. Data Protection

Data is handled under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.
