Terms and Conditions

1. Loan Agreement

By accepting these terms, you enter into a legally binding agreement with SCB Quick Loans (Kenya) Limited, a company licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya.

2. Loan Amount and Interest

The loan amount and interest rate will be displayed in your personalized offer. Interest is calculated on a reducing balance basis at rates between 6.5% and 9% per month, depending on your credit profile.

3. Processing Fee

A non-refundable processing fee is required to cover credit bureau checks, identity verification, and administrative costs. This fee is paid once per application.

4. Repayment

You agree to repay the loan according to the terms selected. Late payments may result in late payment fees and listing with Credit Reference Bureaus.

5. Early Repayment

You may repay the loan early at any time with no penalty. Interest will only be charged for the days you've used the money.

6. Data Protection

Your personal data is collected, stored, and processed in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya.
